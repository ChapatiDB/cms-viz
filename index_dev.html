Visualizing Medicare Provider Charge Data

In 2013, the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) released provider charge data for the top 100 inpatient services and top 30 outpatient services for over 3000 Medicare providers across the United States.

This visualization groups providers by their Hospital Referral Region, showing the average charge to Medicare, average payment from Medicare, and the % reduction from charge to payment for all of these health services.

Mouse over a particular region in any of the maps to see the specific values for that region. Click a region to zoom in. Click again to return to the full map.
